Privacy Policy Doggo Pic Viewer does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. Doggo Pic Viewer is a local image viewer for macOS. The app only accesses image folders that the user manually selects on their Mac. The app does not require an account, does not upload images, does not use analytics, and does not include advertising or tracking. All images remain on the user's device.
