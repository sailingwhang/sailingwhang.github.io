Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Doggo Pic Viewer for macOS.

Overview

Doggo Pic Viewer is a lightweight local image viewer for macOS. The app is designed to help users browse local image folders, manga pages, comics, and visual materials on their Mac.

Doggo Pic Viewer does not collect, store, share, sell, or upload any personal data.

Data Collection

Doggo Pic Viewer does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics data, device identifiers, location data, contact information, or image content.

Local File Access

Doggo Pic Viewer only accesses image folders that the user manually selects on their Mac.

The selected files are used only for local viewing inside the app. Your images remain on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

No Account Required

Doggo Pic Viewer does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

No Advertising or Tracking

Doggo Pic Viewer does not include advertising, third-party analytics, tracking tools, or tracking SDKs.

Internet Connection

Doggo Pic Viewer is designed to work locally. The app does not upload images or user data to the internet.

Children's Privacy

Doggo Pic Viewer does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
