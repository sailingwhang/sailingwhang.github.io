Doggo Pic Viewer Support

Doggo Pic Viewer is a lightweight local image viewer for macOS, designed for browsing manga pages, comics, image folders, and visual materials smoothly on your Mac.

Basic Usage

  • Open the app and choose a local image folder.
  • Use the left and right arrow keys to switch between images.
  • Use the mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom images.
  • When zoomed in, drag the image to view different areas.
  • Your images stay on your Mac and are not uploaded.

Supported Image Types

Doggo Pic Viewer supports common image formats such as JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP, depending on macOS system support.

Troubleshooting

The app cannot see my images.

Please make sure you selected the folder that directly contains the image files. If your images are inside subfolders, open the specific subfolder you want to view.

The image order looks wrong.

Doggo Pic Viewer sorts images by filename. For best results, name your files in a clear order, such as 001.jpg, 002.jpg, 003.jpg.

Zoom or dragging does not feel right.

Try returning the image to the default fit-to-window view, then zoom in again. You can also reopen the folder if needed.
