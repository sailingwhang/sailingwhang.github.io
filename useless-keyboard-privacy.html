Useless Keyboard Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Useless Keyboard respects your privacy.

This app does not require account registration, does not require login, and does not upload your input content to any server.

The text you paste into Type My Text, the content you type inside the app, and the share cards generated by the app are processed locally on your device.

This app does not collect, store, sell, or share your personal information.

This app does not include third-party advertising tracking and does not provide your usage content to third parties.

This app may use the system share feature provided by iOS. When you choose to share an image or text, the sharing action is handled by the system app or third-party app you select. Please also refer to the privacy policy of the corresponding service.

This app may store a small amount of local settings on your device, such as your selected theme, sound on/off status, volume settings, and whether a specific theme has been unlocked. These settings are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

If the app’s features or data usage change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

If you have any questions, please contact: diamedia@live.com
