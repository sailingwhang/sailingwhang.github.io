About the App

Useless Keyboard is not a system keyboard or input method. It is a small, local, relaxing typing toy app.

Text input, typed content, and share card generation are all processed locally on your device.

If you experience issues with sound playback, sharing, themes, or other features, you may try restarting the app first. You can also contact us by email.
